Using Various Copy Functions

 Copying Single Sided Originals onto Both Sides of Single Paper or Both Sided Originals onto Single Sides or Both Sides of Paper

For 2-sided on the print settings screen, the following settings are available: 12sided print, 21sided print, or 22sided print. They provide double-sided copying as shown below.

To change the staple position for both sided originals, or both sided print paper, press the right Function button to select Advanced.
