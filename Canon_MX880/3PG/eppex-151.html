Starting Easy-PhotoPrint EX

  1. From the Go menu of Finder, select Applications, then double-click the Canon Utilities folder, Easy-PhotoPrint EX folder, then Easy-PhotoPrint EX icon.

    Easy-PhotoPrint EX starts and Menu appears.
