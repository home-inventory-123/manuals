Correcting and Enhancing Photos

You can correct and enhance images.

Click (Correct/Enhance Images) in the Select Images or Edit screen, or in the Layout/Print screen of Photo Print. You can make the following corrections and enhancements in the Correct/Enhance Images window.

Important

  • For Photo Print, if you select Enable ICC Profile in the Advanced tab of the Preferences dialog, you cannot correct/enhance images.

Note

  • See "Correct/Enhance Images Window" for details on the Correct/Enhance Images window.
