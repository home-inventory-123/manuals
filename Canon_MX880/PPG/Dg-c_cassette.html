Setting the Paper Allocation

When selecting Quality & Media on the pop-up menu in the Print Dialog, and specifying Paper Allocation as Paper Source, specify the size of the plain paper to be fed from the cassette.

  1. Select Paper Allocation from the pop-up menu on the Canon IJ Printer Utility

  2. Select the paper size

    From Paper Size, select the plain paper size to be fed from the cassette.

    Important

    • The Paper Size box displays only the paper sizes that this product can use.

  3. Complete the setup

    Click Apply, and when the confirmation message appears, click OK.

    The paper size to be printed from the cassette will be changed from hereafter.
